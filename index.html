Who We Are

We are a legal tech company driven by passion to improve lives in India. We started in 2014 with our website OnlineRTI.com to simplify RTI in India. In 2016, we launched CrimeCheck. Today, CrimeCheck is the biggest database of court records in India and is serving many Banks, NBFCs and background verification companies.
